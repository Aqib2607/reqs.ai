## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of our software seriously. If you find a security vulnerability, please report it to us as soon as possible.

### How to Report

Please report security vulnerabilities by emailing us at [security@reqs.ai](mailto:security@reqs.ai).

### What to Include

* A description of the vulnerability.
* Steps to reproduce.
* The impact of the vulnerability.

### What to Expect

* We will respond to your report within 48 hours.
* We will keep you updated on our progress.
* We will notify you when the vulnerability is fixed.
